Tea preparation is the process of turning tea leaves into a beverage. While the particular steps vary with the type of tea and with the raw materials, the process generally includes several steps: tea leaves are harvested, processed, and then brewed to extract flavors.

Tea can be brewed using various methods such as steeping, boiling, or cold brewing. In most areas, tea may be purchased as loose leaves, in tea bags, or as pre-packaged tea blends. Tea is often packaged to ensure freshness and flavor preservation.
