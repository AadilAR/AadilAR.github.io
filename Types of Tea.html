Types of Tea

Drink Preparation Temperature Ingredients Details
Green Tea Latte Matcha Powder Hot Matcha powder, steamed milk, and sweetener (optional) Mix matcha powder with hot water, then add steamed milk and sweetener (if desired)
Chai Latte Chai Concentrate Hot Chai concentrate, steamed milk Mix chai concentrate with steamed milk
Herbal Iced Tea Infusion Cold Herbal tea bags, cold water, sweetener (optional) Steep herbal tea bags in cold water, add sweetener if desired, and serve over ice
Bubble Tea Milk Tea Cold Tea base, milk, tapioca pearls, sweetener Shake together tea base, milk, and sweetener, add tapioca pearls, and serve with a wide straw
Earl Grey Latte Earl Grey Tea Hot Earl Grey tea, steamed milk Steep Earl Grey tea, add steamed milk, and optionally sweeten to taste
Thai Iced Tea Thai Tea Mix Cold Thai tea mix, water, condensed milk, sweetener (optional) Brew Thai tea mix, sweeten with condensed milk and/or sweetener, and serve over ice
